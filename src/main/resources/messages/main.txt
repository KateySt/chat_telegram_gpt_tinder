Hello, this bot combines the convenience of *Telegram*, the power of *ChatGPT* and the fun of *Tinder*.

*The chatbot can:
1. Generate a Tinder profile based on the description 😎.
2. Write interesting and intriguing messages for dating. 😘
3. Conduct correspondence on your behalf 😇🔥.
4. You can practice chatting with a chatbot. 🥰


*Useful commands and links:* 1.
1. /start - the main menu of the bot
2. /profile - generate a Tinder profile 😎.
3. /opener - message to get to know each other 🥰
4. /message - correspondence on your behalf 😈
5. /date - correspondence with the stars 🔥
6. /gpt - ask a question to the GPT chat 🧠
